About me

My full name is Kevin Joseph Doucette and I live in Vernon, Ontario. But that does not appear on google maps, So I just tell people I live in Osgoode, Ontario.

I am currently 29 years old and I’ve been working dead end retail jobs since around 2011, and after being sick and fed‐up with that I decided to go back to school to further my education.

I was not too sure how I would feel about the course. I took a few of those online surveys to find out what college course you should take, and this course, or course's like it, appeared in the top of the list often. The class I was most worried about was this one. I took some coding classes in high school and I tried to learn some game dev coding on my own time and I just could not get into it. Everything just felt to strict. But so far, I am really enjoying this class. It feels more open and freer then the c++ coding I was used to. I’m looking forward to continuing with this course and seeing where it will take me and what other courses will grab me like this one does.
